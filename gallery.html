    Nature & Scenic Views

  1. Chapman's Peak Drive = one of the world's most scenic coastal drives with breathtaking ocean and mountain views.

  2. Hout Bay Beach = wide sandy beach, good for walks, horse riding, or relaxing with views of the Sentinel peak.

  3. The Sentinel Mountain = iconic peak at the mouth of the bay, popular with hikers and photographers.

    4. Wildlife & Outdoor Activities

  4. Seal Island (Duiker Island) = take a boat trip from the harbor to see hundreds of Cape fur seals in their natural habitat.

  5. World of Birds Wildlife Sanctuary = Africa's largest bird park with over 3,000 birds and small animals.

  6. Dungeons = a famous big-wave surf spot, attracting professional surfers and extreme sports fans.
